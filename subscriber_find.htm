Simple form to demonstrate finding which list/s a certain email is subscribed to

* Note to maintain security between your lists you should pass the actual ID of the group that these list/s are part of otherwise all lists in the database will be searched. (e.g Administrator Group = 1 - which will search all lists as administrator has full access, you may then have a ABC Company Administrator Group = 2 and only want to search all lists controlled by ABC Company you woudl then pass GroupID = 2)
